Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Rental Check ("the App") is designed to help you document the condition of rental properties at move-in. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

2. Data We Collect and Store

The App stores the following data only on your device:

  • Property and tenant information you enter (e.g. property address, tenant name, dates of possession and inspection)
  • Your inspection checklists and answers for each room
  • Damage reports you create, including notes and photos you take with the device camera
  • Generated PDF reports

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or third parties. All data remains on your device unless you explicitly share a PDF (e.g. via the system share sheet).

3. Camera Access

The App requests camera access only to capture photos as evidence for damage reports. Photos are stored locally and are not uploaded anywhere unless you choose to share a generated report.

4. Data Retention

Data is stored on your device until you delete it (e.g. by deleting a report in History) or uninstall the App.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with Apple's requirements, you can delete any saved report and its associated data at any time from the History screen. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

6. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The current version is always available in the App under Legal.
